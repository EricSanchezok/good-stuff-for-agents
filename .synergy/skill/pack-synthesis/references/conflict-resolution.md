# Conflict Resolution

Resolve conflicts by preferring the smallest pack that preserves task value.

Resolution options:

1. exclude the weaker or less evidenced skill;
2. assign non-overlapping workflow stages;
3. add explicit sequencing or handoff boundaries;
4. mark the conflict and lower evaluation readiness;
5. split into separate pack candidates.

Document all high-ranking excluded skills with an exclusion reason. Never hide a known conflict to reach the publication threshold.
